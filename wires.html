Problem Statement

WIRES users find it difficult to source relevant information on he website due to unclear labeling and dense information. How might we make it clearer and easier for users to find relevant information on WIRES's website?

Research

1. Identifying problems

I began the research phase by conducting heuristic analysis to identify existing problems with WIRES's website. Here, I found some key issues with its color schemes, content, navigation and functionality.

Color Accessibility

Using WebAIM's contrast checker, I found that over 60% of WIRES' color combination failed accessiblity standards.

Navigation

Header Navigation was hidden in a burger menu, with 10 different tabs and no indication of the page the user is at.

Content

content is text-heavy with very little images. Some sections contain long paragraphs and several hyperlinks.
